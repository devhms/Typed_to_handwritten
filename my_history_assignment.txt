History Assignment: The Industrial Revolution

The Industrial Revolution marked a major turning point in history. It began in the late 18th century in Great Britain and fundamentally changed how people lived and worked. 

Before the revolution, most goods were made by hand in small workshops or at home. The introduction of steam power and new machinery allowed for mass production in large factories. This led to a significant shift from rural agrarian societies to urban industrial ones.

However, the revolution also brought significant challenges. Working conditions in factories were often poor, and cities grew rapidly without adequate housing or sanitation. Despite these issues, the technological advancements of the era laid the foundation for the modern world.
